Zero-shot Emotion Evaluation System for Videos Using Multi-modal Methods and LLMs


David Yang / 2023 December

Emotion Evaluation Video Analysis Multi-modal LLMs

This research tackles the challenge of assessing emotion intensity in videos. Utilizing multi-modal feature extraction and Large Language Models (LLMs) feature analysis, we introduce an innovative system for estimating emotion intensity in videos. In experiments encompassing diverse video genres, our system demonstrated high performance, achieving an accuracy of 86.4% in estimating emotion intensities. These results affirm the effectiveness and generalization capability of our proposed system.



*Note: This is a master capstone project collaborated with my teammates Navneet Ratnakirti, Siqi Chen, Yongxiang Chen. The project is in partnership with Wattch Fitness, and supervised by Prof. Lino Coria Mendoza.
