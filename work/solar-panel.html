Global Solar Panel Farms Mapping through Multispectral Satellite Imagery and Enhanced Swin Transformer Model


David Yang / 2023 December

Solar Energy Multispectral Satellite Imagery Remote Sensing Swin Transformer

Solar Photovoltaic (PV) technology significantly impacts the world by providing clean energy without emitting greenhouse gases, helping combat climate change and reduce pollution. Monitoring solar panel farms is crucial for understanding the global transition to clean energy.


Our project aims to create a comprehensive multispectralimage dataset of solar panel farms, enhancing segmentation models. We will propose an improved Swin Transformer Model - Sequential Spectral Swin (SSSwin) tailored for efficient handling of multispectral data. Our goal is to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of solar panel farms mapping, advancing solar energy technology globally.



*Note: This is research project is directed and collaborated with Prof. Ryan Rad.
