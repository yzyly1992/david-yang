Plant Diary - Unfold the Beauty of Your Favorite Plants


David Yang / 2023 May

Mobile Dev React Native Expo Firebase GoogleMaps API PlantId API

Slogan: "Unfold the Beauty of Your Favorite Plants: Share their Journey with Plant Diary App!"


Are you a plant enthusiast looking to foster your love for growing plants but uncertain about where to find like-minded individuals and how to kickstart your journey? Look no further than the Plant Diary App – it's tailor-made for you! This app serves as a vibrant social platform and community exclusively dedicated to plant lovers. With a user-friendly interface and powerful features, it enables you to connect with fellow enthusiasts, share your greenery triumphs, explore a diverse range of plants, and engage with a community that shares your passion.


Key Features

  • Thriving Plant Community: Our app hosts a passionate and engaged community of plant lovers from all walks of life. Connect, interact, and exchange knowledge with individuals who share your enthusiasm for cultivating and caring for plants.
  • Plant Sharing: Share your plant-growing journey effortlessly with the community. Upload pictures, jot down notes about your plants' progress, and receive encouraging feedback from fellow enthusiasts.
  • Discover New Plants: Get inspired by a vast collection of plants curated by the community. Discover new species, learn about their unique traits, and explore a world of botanical wonders.
  • Plant Identification: Are you unsure about the name of a plant you just encountered? Use the app's plant identification feature to quickly find out its name and learn more about it.
  • Achievement System: recognise the user’s effort and encourage them to continue using the app for getting more achievement.
